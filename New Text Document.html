British Shorthair Cat Breed

About the British Shorthair

The British Shorthair is a pedigreed cat breed originating in the United Kingdom. It has a sturdy build, dense coat, broad face, and big round eyes.

Characteristics

  • Temperament: Easygoing, affectionate
  • Coat: Short, dense, plush
  • Colors: Blue, black, white, cream, and more
  • Size: Medium to large
